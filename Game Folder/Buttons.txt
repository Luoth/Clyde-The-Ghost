Because this game was made to be played on an arcade, the button layout for keyboard isn't made clear in the game.
Thus, here are the buttons:

Player 1 (larry)
Orange button player1: E
Movement Up: W
Movement Right: D
Movement Left: A
Movement Down: S

Player 2 (ghost/clyde)
Orange button: L
Movement Up: up arrow
Movement Right: right arrow
Movement Left: left arrow
Movement Down: down arrow
Shooting: 0 on num pad
